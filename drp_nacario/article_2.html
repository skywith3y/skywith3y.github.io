Face-to-face or face-to-screen?

This piece discusses the shift from traditional face-to-face interactions to digital, screen-based communication, analyzing its effects on interpersonal relationships.

We examine how virtual interactions compare to in-person conversations in terms of emotional depth, understanding, and the development of social skills.
