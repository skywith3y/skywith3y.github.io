Dengue Prevention Tips

Follow these guidelines to protect yourself and your community from dengue.

Personal Protection

  • ✔ Use insect repellent (DEET or Picaridin).
  • ✔ Wear long-sleeved clothing and pants.
  • ✔ Sleep under mosquito nets.

At Home

  • ✔ Empty stagnant water from containers.
  • ✔ Clean gutters & remove mosquito breeding sites.
  • ✔ Use window screens and close doors properly.

Community Efforts

  • ✔ Participate in community clean-up drives.
  • ✔ Report dengue cases to local authorities.
  • ✔ Encourage neighbors to remove standing water.

Travel Safety

  • ✔ Apply repellent before going outdoors.
  • ✔ Avoid mosquito-infested areas.
  • ✔ Stay in places with air conditioning or screens.

Recognizing Symptoms

  • ✔ Know the early symptoms of dengue.
  • ✔ Seek medical help if symptoms persist.
  • ✔ Avoid self-medication with aspirin/NSAIDs.

Emergency Plan

  • ✔ Have emergency contacts ready.
  • ✔ Keep oral rehydration salts available.
  • ✔ Head to the nearest hospital if needed.
