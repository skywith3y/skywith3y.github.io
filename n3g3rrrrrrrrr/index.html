About the BSP

The Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) is the central bank of the Republic of the Philippines. Established on July 3, 1993, under the 1987 Philippine Constitution and the New Central Bank Act, the BSP assumed the responsibilities of the former Central Bank of the Philippines (founded in 1949).

As the country’s central monetary authority, the BSP is entrusted with promoting financial stability and price stability. It operates with full fiscal and administrative autonomy in pursuit of its mandate.
