What is Dengue?

Dengue is the fastest spreading vector-borne disease in the world endemic in 100 countries.

The disease is prevalent in tropical and subtropical areas, including the Philippines, where it poses a significant public health challenge. Dengue virus has four serotypes (DENV1, DENV2, DENV3 and DENV4). Dengue has no treatment but the disease can be early managed.
